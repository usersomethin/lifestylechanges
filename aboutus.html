About Us

We worked together as a team utilizing our specifically chosen skills to help with this project. This website represents the result of months of research, discussion and teamwork focusing on a global issue that we believe is critical to our world today.

Why This Topic?

We chose this topic because while surfing the internet one of our team members found out that there are a lot of beliefs that have been followed from generations and are being passed on to the future. After that, we got the opportunity to find out more about it by the help of this project. We all have had some beliefs and superstitions that have been passed on to us but some of us didn't even know what they meant. So, we wanted to find out more about it and share our findings with the world. We also wanted to know how these beliefs have changed over time and how they have been adapted to modern lifestyles. We believe that understanding these changes can help us appreciate the importance of preserving cultural heritage while embracing modern advancements.
